Government Health Schemes

Ayushman Bharat

Provides health coverage up to ₹5 lakhs per family per year for secondary and tertiary care hospitalization.

  • Covers pre and post hospitalization expenses
  • No restriction on family size
  • All pre-existing conditions covered from day one

CGHS (Central Government Health Scheme)

Comprehensive health care facility for Central Government employees and pensioners.

  • OPD and Indoor treatment coverage
  • Includes family members
  • Access to CGHS wellness centers

Rastriya Arogya Nidhi

Financial assistance for treatment of major life-threatening diseases for patients living below poverty line.

  • Cancer treatment support up to ₹2 lakhs
  • Treatment at government hospitals
  • Direct financial assistance to hospitals

Janani Shishu Suraksha Karyakram

Free and cashless services to pregnant women and sick newborns.

  • Free delivery and C-section
  • Free drugs and consumables
  • Free diagnostics and transport

Long Term Fellowship (HRD)

Fellowship program for health research and development in medical sciences.

  • Research funding support
  • Monthly stipend
  • Academic development opportunities

Health Minister's Discretionary Grant

Financial assistance for patients with rare diseases and critical illnesses.

  • Case-by-case financial support
  • Coverage for rare diseases
  • Emergency medical support

Nikshay Poshan Yojana

Nutritional support for TB patients through Direct Benefit Transfer.

  • ₹500 per month nutritional support
  • Direct bank transfer
  • Support throughout treatment duration
